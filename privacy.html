1. 📊 Information We Collect

Device Information

We collect limited device information (such as device model, OS version, app version, and crash data) only when you voluntarily submit the in-app "Report a Problem" or "Feedback" forms. This information helps us diagnose technical issues and improve app performance.

Third-Party Analytics Data

Our third-party service providers may automatically collect anonymized usage data and analytics information to help us understand how the app is used and improve user experience.

What We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or have access to:

  • Your expense entries, income records, or financial data
  • Your budgets, categories, or account balances
  • Personal information like names, emails, or contact details (unless you provide them in feedback)
  • Device location, camera, contacts, SMS, or other sensitive device features
  • Your browsing history or usage from other apps

All your financial data is stored locally on your device only and never leaves your device.

2. ⚙️ How We Use Your Information

We use the limited information we collect to:

  • Troubleshoot technical problems you report through feedback forms
  • Improve app performance, reliability, and user experience
  • Analyze anonymized usage patterns to enhance app features
  • Respond to your support requests and feedback

We do not:

  • Sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Use your information for targeted advertising beyond standard app monetization
  • Access or transmit your financial data from your device

3. 💾 Data Storage & Retention

Your Financial Data

  • Stored locally on your device only - we have no access to this information
  • Remains on your device until you delete the app or clear app data
  • Can be exported or backed up using app features (data remains under your control)
  • We cannot recover your data if lost - please back up important information

Feedback & Support Data

  • Information submitted through feedback forms is retained only as long as necessary to address your inquiry
  • Typically deleted within 30 days after resolving technical issues
  • May be retained longer if needed for ongoing app improvements

4. 🔗 Third-Party Services

The app integrates with third-party services that have their own privacy policies and may collect information independently:

Services We Use:

  • Google AdMob - For displaying advertisements
  • Firebase Analytics - For app usage analytics and performance monitoring
  • Firebase Crashlytics - For crash reporting and app stability analysis
  • Firestore - For essential app functionality (no personal financial data stored)

Privacy Policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy and Security

These third-party services may collect device identifiers, usage data, and other information as described in their respective privacy policies. We do not control their data collection practices.

5. 🔒 Data Safety & Security

Your Device Security

  • Your financial data security depends on your device's built-in protection
  • We recommend using device lock screens, regular OS updates, and security best practices
  • Keep your device secure as all your financial information is stored locally

Data We Handle

  • We implement reasonable security measures to protect information transmitted through feedback forms
  • Communications with our support team use secure channels
  • No method of electronic transmission is 100% secure - we cannot guarantee absolute security

6. 👶 Children's Privacy

Kashtraq is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at kashtraqsup@gmail.com and we will promptly investigate and delete such information.

7. ⚖️ Your Rights & Choices

Complete Control Over Your Data

  • Delete all data: Uninstall the app or clear app data through device settings
  • Export data: Use in-app export features to back up your financial information
  • Limit data sharing: Choose not to submit feedback or problem reports
  • Manage ads: Control ad preferences through your Google account settings

Third-Party Services

  • Opt out of analytics through your device's privacy settings
  • Manage ad personalization in your Google account
  • Review and adjust permissions in your device settings

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. When we make changes, we will:

  • Post the revised policy in the app and on our distribution channels
  • Continue using the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
